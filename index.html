Welcome to my fabulous etch-a-sketch web-app.

Draw by holding down x.

Select a different color by holding down c, hover over the color of your choice and release c.

Toggle pen transpanecy with z

If you wish to see this window again, press h.

toggle special hue with v (feature only works with transparency enabled)

If you wish to change the resolution of the canvas, enter a number between 2 and 100 in the field below and click reset.
